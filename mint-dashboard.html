Venue types & default setup times

Picking a venue in the Add-order form fills the setup time automatically. Changing a default here applies to new orders (existing orders keep their own setup time, editable in the schedule).

New-order defaults

Used when an order doesn't bring its own values. Venue types override the delivery setup. Per-order values entered in the schedule always win.

In-house fuel cost

Diesel cost for your own trucks. UAE sets the diesel price monthly — update it here when it changes. A 5-ton truck doing 100 L per 500 km uses 20 L/100km. The day summary shows the in-house fuel cost from the kilometres driven.

Crew & staffing

Your in-house crew (assigned truck crew + spare warehouse crew, added in the Crew section) are counted against each day's busiest crew need. When you're short, the day summary shows how many to order from a manpower-supply company and the cost.
